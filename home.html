Our services

Our services

Vinte is a professional website design and development studio based in Argentina. We create human experience in a digital world; using the best practices and latest web standards guidelines. The results are digital experiences lovingly hand coded by combining business strategy, identity, UX/UI, and content.

We design.

We design websites that shows the spirit of our clients, without forgetting the good functioning of the site in terms of user interaction.

We brand.

A brand is much more than a simple logo. A brand says it all by itself. We design your identity system so that it represents the ideals of your company.

We build.

We use the latest technologies and create incredible responsive websites, adapted to any size of screen. We have skills in HTML(5), CSS(3), SASS, jQuery, React.js, Ruby on Rails.
